Our Story

Brilliance with a conscience.

Jivani Gems is a modern atelier crafting laboratory-grown diamonds with the patience of mined stones — and the conscience of the next century.

The Beginning

A diamond should never cost the earth.

We started Jivani Gems with a simple conviction: the brilliance of a diamond and the integrity of how it’s made should never be in conflict. Lab-grown diamonds gave us both — identical fire and fact, grown in weeks rather than torn from the ground over millennia.

Every piece is designed in-house, set in 100% recycled precious metal, and independently certified by IGI or GIA. The result is jewelry you can wear — and pass on — without compromise.

Why Lab-Grown

Chemically identical. Ethically superior.

A lab-grown diamond is a diamond — the same crystal of pure carbon, the same hardness, the same dispersion of light. The only difference is the address where it was born.

  • Identical optical, physical and chemical properties to mined diamonds
  • Graded on the same 4Cs by the same laboratories
  • Typically a fraction of the price — choose a larger, finer stone
  • No mining, fully traceable origin, recycled metals
